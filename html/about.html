ABOUT BRENDEN



Brenden Wishlow

Multimedia Designer

About Brenden

Brenden was born and raised in Saskatchewan. He went to the University of Regina and earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree in Film & Video Production with a Minor in Theatre Arts.

After university, he moved to Toronto to pursue a career in Production Design. He has worked on films that have premiered at festivals throughout the world – BFI Flare (UK), Atlanta Film Festival (USA), Whistler Film Festival (Canada). Brenden recently graduated from the Interactive Media Management program at Algonquin College in Ottawa, where he made the Dean’s Honour List. The program gave him a strong foundation in UX, Web, and Graphic Design.

Brenden’s combination of education and experience gives him a well-rounded set of skills and makes him a valuable asset for any team. He is excited to put his creative and technical skills towards designing an accessible and engaging user experience.

In his spare time, Brenden likes to explore new places, play tennis, and walk his dog, Nukki.
